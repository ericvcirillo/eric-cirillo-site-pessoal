Serasa Studio, Identity & Fraud
React · Node.js · REST APIs · SDKs · Claude Code

Internal platform for testing, demonstrations, and troubleshooting of Serasa Experian's Identity & Fraud products. Conceived, architected, and delivered solo in roughly three weeks using AI agents as a development accelerator, turning years of operational knowledge into a tool used daily by Professional Services and Sales.

  • Solves a real problem. Before it, each specialist used a different tool and demos depended on whoever knew Postman. Studio centralizes testing, demos, troubleshooting, flows, and knowledge base in a single environment.

  • Includes Demo mode with no credentials, Onboarding Builder that runs complete journeys and generates a PDF report, and Flow Builder for anti-fraud domain diagrams.
  • Recognized by leadership as one of the most relevant initiatives presented to the internal AI Committee. Approved for corporate publication.
